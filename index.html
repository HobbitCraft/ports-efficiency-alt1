Method and sources

Success is the lowest of Morale, Combat, and Seafaring percentages. Each crew level adds 10% of the unmodified base value, so level 10 is exactly double level 0. Solidarity is calculated from unique crew types plus the captain. The app never clicks or changes RuneScape.
